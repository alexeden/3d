# Summary

This is a remix of the awesome Maya Skull design
https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:1755540

Adapted for my custom designed Death Whistle Resonator. Its designed to be very strong, easy to print and very loud.

Worn for ceremonies and taken into battle to intimidate enemies by recreating their death screams as a sign of their impending death. Read more about Death Whistles here 
http://www.mexicolore.co.uk/aztecs/music/death-whistle

If you use supports ensure Touching Platform ONLY

Files: 
STL
Creality CR10 gcode PLA direct print
Ideamaker idea File
Ideamaker Filament Profile

# Print Settings

Printer: Creality CR-10S
Rafts: No
Supports: Doesn't Matter
Resolution: 0.1 or 0.2
Infill: 25%

Notes: 
You can use support (Touching Platform ONLY) if you want a cleaner print around the cheek bones but its not necessary.

# Post-Printing

No real post processing needed, if you have any stringing your may want to clear the air hole with a small drill bit by hand.
